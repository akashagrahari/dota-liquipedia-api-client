About Tiers[edit]

Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 and Tier 4 are the tiers for the most important tournaments on this wiki. They are categorized as follows:

Name Description
Tier 1 Former Premier. Tier 1 tournaments offer an outstanding prize pool, are almost exclusively played offline, and feature the best teams from all over the world.
Tier 2 Former Major. Tier 2 tournaments feature a large prize pool and a good number of top-tier teams.
Tier 3 Former Minor. Tier 3 tournaments offer a smaller prize pool and less prestige than Tier 2 tournaments but still draw a high level of competition.
Tier 4 Tier 4 tournaments are the lowest ranked events with no top teams participating.

While prize pool is a factor for categorizing tournaments, it does not equal a specific tier.
